About us

Genlab is based in Hong Kong, a leading global centre for commerce, finance and technology. Our multinational team combines expertise in data science, entrepreneurship, engineering, IOT, finance and law. Together, we are committed to catalyzing the learning and use of technology to benefit everyone.


Our current projects include creating platforms for the most effective distribution of technology courseware and applying machine learning techniques to medical diagnosis. For these projects, Genlab is actively integrating data science techniques to continuously improve and optimize methodologies and to ultimately achieve superior results.


Today, these techniques are on the cutting edge of technological advancement in a wide range of fields from internet search to self driving cars and beyond. We at Genlab endeavor to become a leading contributor in this advancement.
